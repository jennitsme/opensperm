Kenapa Opensperm?

Sandbox ketat
Egress allowlist, rlimits CPU/mem, skill ter-signing.
Policy & Approval
Scopes, RBAC/ABAC, approval hook (file/env; extensible prompt/webhook).
Observability
IPC dengan error codes; log/trace per tool call; replay transcript.
Typed SDK
TS/Rust schemas untuk manifest, IPC; contract tests (on progress).
Local-first
Golden transcripts, offline dev, deterministic fixtures.
